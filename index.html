About Us

S-VYASA University (Deemed-to-be University), Sattva Global City Campus, Bangalore

S-VYASA University, School of Advanced Studies, Sattva Global City, Bangalore, was established in 2024 as part of the S-VYASA Society. The university was founded by Padma Shri awardee Dr. H. R. Nagendra. It offers a range of programmes across disciplines such as Engineering, Computer Applications, Business & Management, and Allied Health, catering to students at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels.

The core objective of S-VYASA University Sattva Global City Campus is to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in their chosen fields by integrating academic learning with real-world applications. The university strives to drive positive change and shape the future of society by fostering curiosity, promoting research excellence, and encouraging innovation across diverse domains.
